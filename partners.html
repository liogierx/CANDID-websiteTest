CANDID study partners



End of 2021, the Loulou Foundation, announced that seven biopharmaceutical industry partners, together with the Loulou Foundation, had formed a pre-competitive consortium to direct a key clinical study for the development of disease-modifying therapeutics for CDKL5 Deficiciency Disorder (CDD).

The seven companies are Amicus Therapeutics; Biogen Inc.; Elaaj Bio; Marinus Pharmaceuticals Inc. (now Immedica AB); PTC Therapeutics; Ultragenyx Pharmaceuticals Inc. and Zogenix Inc. (now UCB). The seven partners share the funding and governance of a 3-year observational study with CDD patients, called CANDID. The Loulou Foundation serves as the study coordinator.
